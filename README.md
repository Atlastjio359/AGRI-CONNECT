# 🌾 Agriconnect

### *Bridg between the Farmers & Shopkeepers for a Sustainable Agricultural Future*

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**Agriconnect** is a  web platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers, shopkeepers, and agricultural experts. It empowers farmers with the right tools, knowledge, and products to make informed, sustainable decisions—enhancing crop yields while minimizing environmental impact.

> *"From soil to sale—Agriconnect connects the agricultural ecosystem."*

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers face critical challenges:
- ❌ Lack of timely access to expert agricultural advice
- ❌ Difficulty finding the right fertilizers and products
- ❌ Communication gaps between farmers and shopkeepers
- ❌ Limited awareness of sustainable farming practices
- ❌ Product shortages and delayed availability

**Agriconnect solves these problems by creating a unified digital ecosystem.**

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🧑‍🌾 For Farmers
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Fertilizer Finder** | Search and discover suitable organic & inorganic fertilizers |
| **Expert Advice** | Get personalized crop production and protection guidance |
| **Weather Forecast** | Real-time weather updates for better planning |
| **Product Information** | Detailed product info with video tutorials |
| **Direct Call** | One-tap communication with experts and shopkeepers |

### 🏪 For Shopkeepers
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Product Management** | Showcase available fertilizers and products |
| **Price Updates** | Real-time pricing updates |
| **Direct Communication** | Receive calls and queries directly from farmers |

### 👨‍🔬 For Experts
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Advice Platform** | Share expert knowledge on crop management |
| **Sustainable Practices** | Promote organic and eco-friendly farming |

---

## 📊 Survey & Field Research

Our team conducted extensive field research in Amravati District to understand real-world challenges:

**Key Findings:**
- ⚠️ Product shortages causing delays
- 📞 Farmers need direct communication with shopkeepers
- 💡 Shopkeepers requested call feature for better coordination
- 🌱 High demand for organic fertilizer information
